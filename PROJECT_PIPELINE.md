## Notes and Lessons

### What Works

- **GEO optimization** â€” 95+ scores require upfront structure planning, not post-hoc fixes

- **Team strategy articles** require more data aggregation (longer dev time)
- **GEO scores** are highly sensitive to heading structure and entity clarity
